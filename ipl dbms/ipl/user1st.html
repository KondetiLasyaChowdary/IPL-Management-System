About Cricket

Cricket is a bat-and-ball sport played between two teams, each consisting of eleven players. The game is widely known for its strategic gameplay, where one team bats to score runs, while the other team bowls and fields to prevent scoring. Matches are typically played in various formats, including Test cricket, One Day Internationals (ODIs), and Twenty20 (T20) matches, making it one of the most popular sports globally. Cricket has a rich history, with iconic tournaments like the ICC Cricket World Cup and Indian Premier League (IPL) capturing the enthusiasm of fans worldwide.

TATA Group secures title sponsorship rights for IPL 2024-28The BCCI on Saturday awarded the title sponsorship rights for the Indian Premier League to TATA Group for a period of five years.
